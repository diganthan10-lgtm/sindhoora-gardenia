Our story

A table set for every generation

Sindhoora Gardenia began as a promise: that a family could gather, order what each person actually craves, and leave the table happier than they arrived. Across our Bengaluru outposts, that promise has grown into an upscale, family-styled dining tradition built on unhurried service and kitchens that are watched over as closely as a home hearth.

Soft recessed lighting, quiet music, and rooms built for long conversation set the mood — while the food carries the real weight of the evening, drawing from Andhra, coastal, tandoor, oriental and continental kitchens under one roof.
